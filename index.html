Helping Chicago's Youth Discover Code

Chicago's center for high school coding meetups and makeathons

Makeathons ->

Makeathons are a twist on the original hackathon model to make them more accessible. It is a 12 hour long hackathon-like event that is focused on creating things with technology. Students are encouraged to work collaboratively in groups with their friends. Throughout the event there are fun workshops where students who range from beginners in coding to advanced can learn about various technologies. At the end, those projects are judged and the most creative, innovative, or beneficial projects win.

Young Developer Meetups ->

Calling all middle and high school students! You'll feel right at home whether you're a software guru or have never touched a computer. You don't have to be a "math person" or a "techy person" to join: all we ask is that you bring a desire and passion to learn new things. Led by students like you, we are dedicated to connecting and empowering young people to write code. Our meetups are split into two sections: first we have hour-long workshops that teach some part of coding, and then we split into groups and work together on different projects.
